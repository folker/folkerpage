Folker Meyer's homepage

Folker Meyer is a Professor of Data science at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence at University hospital Essen part of the University of Duisburg-Essen Germany.

Folker trained as a computer scientists and started to work with biologists early on in his career. It was that exposure to interesting biological and bio-medical problems that sparked his interest in building software systems to solve hard biological problems, mostly in the field of genomics or post-genomics. He is best known for his role in the development of large scale data analysis systems like: GenDB, RAST and MG-RAST. He has also played an active role in the design and implementation of several high-performance computing platforms.

His interest include various data science, microbiome science, microbial genomics, microbial ecology as well as distributed and cloud computing.

He is a founding member of the Earthmicrobiome project (EMP) and a board member of the Genomics Standards Consortium (GSC). Folker currently serves as the treasurer for the GSC.
